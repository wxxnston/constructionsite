Quality Assurance

Our prime objective is to provide services in a manner which conform to contractual and regulatory requirements. In order to achieve this objective, it is our policy to establish and maintain an efficient
Quality Assurance programme planned and developed in conjunction with all management functions inline with ISO 9001 and 9002 requirements.




Community Relations


Our Corporate Community Engagement (CCE) strategy is built on native insights and deep knowledge of relationship management between other construction companies and their host communities in the Nigerian oil and gas industry; aimed at eliciting convivial stakeholder relationship that is built on the principle of shared responsibility for mutual benefits. We have over 22-years' experience and history of smoothening relations within the communities we have operated in; interfacing with the different community demographics, community leadership hierarchy, governments and the third sector (NGOs, CBOs, FBOs, YBOs) etc.
