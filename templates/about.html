About Us

RINE EDH INTEGRATED SERVICES LIMITED is an indigenous Limited Liability Company incorporated in 2012 with RC 1040277 under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. It is registered with the Department of Petroleum Resources of the Nigerian National Corporation, Nipex, NJQS and the Inland Revenue Service.

The company was established to provide Engineering Services (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical/ Instrumentation), Environmental Services (Remediation, Cleanup, Containment & Recovery of Free Phase Hydrocarbon, Waste Management, Tank Cleaning and Corrosion Control) and broad based Consultancies in the Oil & Gas sector.

Community Relations


Our Corporate Community Engagement (CCE) strategy is built on native insights and deep knowledge of relationship management between oil and gas companies and their host communities in the Nigerian oil and gas industry; aimed at eliciting convivial stakeholder relationship that is built on the principle of shared responsibility for mutual benefits. We have over 22-years' experience and history of smoothening relations within the communities we have operated in; interfacing with the different community demographics, community leadership hierarchy, governments and the third sector (NGOs, CBOs, FBOs, YBOs) etc.


As a service organization, we are poised to provide cost effective and quality engineering services that meets our clients' expectations.
